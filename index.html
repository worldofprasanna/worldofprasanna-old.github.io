Hi Friends ...

Wass up ? - I had created a notifier app

What it ll notify about ? - About Long weekends and Festivals, well before it ... more precisely before 121 days.

What to do with it ? - Go and book your train ticket through Irctc.

Its as simple as that. Nothing more, Nothing less. Just a reminder service.

How it works ?

We have a predefined list of Long weekends and festivals. We know about IRCTC rules, that the ticket will be opened before 120 days. We would send notification exactly 121 days before the festival, so it would be easy for you to book the ticket. Simple.

Eg : Ganesh Chaturthi is on - Sept 17, 2015 Thu. So we would notify you by May 18, 2015 and you can book train ticket on the next day for Sept 16, 2015.
